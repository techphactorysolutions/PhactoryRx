What this app is and is not
Good for: medication lookup by ingredient, brand, strength, or dosage form; matched uses and descriptions; pharmacology studying; RxNorm identifiers; personal notes; and non-clinical review of label-listed interaction language. Not for: diagnosis, prescribing, dose adjustment, treatment or emergency decisions, or replacing a pharmacist, physician, poison control center, official prescribing information, or clinical drug-interaction software. RxNav’s former drug-drug interaction service was discontinued; the Interactions tab reviews public label text only.

This product uses publicly available data from the U.S. National Library of Medicine, National Institutes of Health, Department of Health and Human Services; NLM is not responsible for the product and does not endorse or recommend this or any other product. FDA label data can vary by product and may be incomplete, changed, delayed, or unavailable through public APIs.
