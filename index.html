Study-grade medication reference

Look up medications by ingredient, brand, strength, or dosage form and get matched uses and a description.

PhactoryRx resolves the selected RxNorm medication concept first, then displays uses and description only from a sufficiently matched human-drug label. It is an educational reference tool, not medical advice, diagnosis, prescribing guidance, or emergency care.

Scope & safety

What this app is and is not

Good for

Medication lookup by ingredient, brand, strength, or dosage form; matched uses and descriptions; pharmacology studying; RxNorm identifiers; personal notes; and non-clinical review of label-listed interaction language.

Not for

Diagnosis, prescribing, dose adjustment, treatment decisions, emergency decisions, or replacing a pharmacist, physician, poison control center, official prescribing information, or clinical drug-interaction software.

This product uses publicly available data from the U.S. National Library of Medicine, National Institutes of Health, Department of Health and Human Services; NLM is not responsible for the product and does not endorse or recommend this or any other product. FDA label data can vary by product and may be incomplete, changed, delayed, or unavailable through public APIs.
